MUSIC

Music is something that we all experience at some point in our lives. Our parents and instructors teach us songs as youngsters as a way of learning a language or simply for fun. Children like singing with others, and it appears that the act of singing in a group fosters a sense of community among participants of all ages. People's musical tastes change as they get older, and we begin to regard our favorite songs as part of our personal histories. In a manner that words alone cannot, music conveys and arouses emotions. In a nutshell, it's hard to fathom living without it.

Jeremy Zucker
